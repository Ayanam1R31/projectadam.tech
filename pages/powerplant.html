ADAM — Powerplant System

ADAM Powerplant

Thrust and power in one simple package.

Overview

A Hybrid Microtubine never attempted before.

The ADAM powerplant is built for any UAV platform that demands eVTOL, ISR, high speed flight, and long range in a single system. It integrates directly into your existing bus, replacing battery power entirely while delivering outsized thrust and continuous electrical output.

Connect your fuel line, bus, and throttle command, and you're ready to fly. Specify your voltage and amperage, and ADAM delivers a complete, integrated propulsion and power system built to your spec.

Capabilities

The limit is your creativity

eVTOL

Combined jet thrust and onboard electrical generation give the ability for electric motor propulsion using propellers.

ISR & Denied Navigation

Surplus electrical output powers onboard sensors and computing without depending on external signals like GPS.

Electronic Warfare

Continuous onboard power generation enables high draw payloads for signal intercept, jamming, or spectrum denial.

Package Logistics

High thrust to weight ratio and electrical output support heavier payloads over longer ranges.
