ADAM — UAV System

Lilith UAV

Fast, rapidly deployable, and versatile.

Overview

The UAV that has it all.

LILITH is a 18kg fin-stabilized VTOL UAV powered by the ADAM hybrid microturbine system, combining vertical takeoff with sustained high speed cruise in one airframe. It reaches Mach 0.80 and roughly 140 km range without the weight penalty of large battery packs.

Built for rapid strike, ISR loiter, and high power payload missions, LILITH takes off from constrained sites and transitions to fixed wing performance in flight, all while ADAM's onboard generation powers sensors and payloads without ground infrastructure.

Flight Architecture

Three States. One Platform.

Lilith transitions between any state on demand — no landing, no reconfiguration required.

01

Vertical Takeoff & Landing

eVTOL

Props vertical, full electric lift. Zero runway required. Precise station-keeping with active stabilization in any environment.

02

Drone-Assisted Flight

Precision

Quadcopter-level control authority at low speed. Exact positioning and attitude hold across all axes — as precise as any multirotor.

03

> Mach 0.3 — Props Fold

Cruise

Above Mach 0.3, props fold flush for a clean aerodynamic profile. High-speed sustained cruise with no drag penalty.
